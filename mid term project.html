Introduction

Hi! I’m Dhairyansh, an undergraduate student at IIT Bombay. Stepping onto campus for the first time was a surreal moment — a blend of nervous excitement and dreams ready to unfold.

Over time, IITB has become more than just an institute to me; it’s been a place of learning, late-night discussions, countless cans of monster and redbull, and friendships that feel like family. This site is a small window into that experience — a timeline of growth, experiences, and the little things that have shaped my life here.
